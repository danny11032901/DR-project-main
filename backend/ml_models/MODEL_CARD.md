# RetinaIQ Model Card

## Model
EfficientNetV2-B3 primary classifier for 5-class diabetic retinopathy grading.

## Intended Use
Clinical decision support for retinal screening workflows. Not a standalone diagnostic tool.

## Metrics
Provide Accuracy, AUC, F1-macro, and QWK after training on approved dataset splits.

## Limitations
Performance can degrade on low-quality or out-of-distribution retinal images.

## Safety
Predictions must be reviewed by a qualified ophthalmologist.
